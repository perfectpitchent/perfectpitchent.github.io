About us

Perfect Pitch Entertainment is an artist-owned audio post production studio with locations available throughout Los Angeles. We create and refine sounds to tell your story with some of the industry's best and most experienced talent!

Perfect Pitch Entertainment always hits the right note in your soundtrack. We've mixed commercials, new media, features, short films, television shows, music, live shows, and podcasts. We know that quality audio is essential to all great works; therefore, we put our hearts into every project, no matter how big or small. The Perfect Pitch team's commitment to collaboration and creativity will help you shape your story through sound.

Our audio suites are equipped with state-of-the-art, industry leading, hardware and software allowing us to create the perfect mix for your project. Our inventive sound designers will breathe life and emotion into your vision with innovative and thoughtful sound creations. Our engineers will work with you to optimize and customize your mix for distribution.
