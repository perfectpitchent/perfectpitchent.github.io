About us

Perfect Pitch Entertainment is an artist-owned audio post production studio with locations available throughout Los Angeles. We create and refine sounds for any medium with some of the industry's best and most experienced talent to tell your story!

Perfect Pitch Entertainment always hits the right note in your soundtrack, whether mixing commercials, new media, features, television, music, or podcasts. We know that quality audio is essential to all great works; therefore, we put our hearts into every project, no matter how big or small. The Perfect Pitch team's commitment to collaboration and creativity will help you shape your story through sound.

Our audio suites have the most cutting-edge equipment with state-of-the-art hardware and software to create the perfect mix for your project. Our inventive sound designers will breathe life into your vision with innovative and thoughtful sound creations. Our engineers will work with you to optimize and customize your mix for distribution.
